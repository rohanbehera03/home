Obesity Data Analysis

In this project, I took a dataset from UCI's machine learning repository that had obesity statistics from individuals in Mexico, Colombia, and Peru. I cleaned the data by binning the amount of water consumed, physical activity, and meals consumed per day and rounding age, height, and weight to the nearest decimal place. I performed exploratory data analysis by creating heat maps, bar charts, and density plots. I used algorithms including k-nearest-neighbors, decision trees, and k-means clustering to predict the obesity level in an individual.

Analyzing Fatal Police Shootings in the U.S.

In this project I took a dataset from the Washington Post looking at fatal police shootings in the U.S. and cleaned it by dropped columns such as location precision, race source, and the agency id. I worked on preprocessing the data by including visualizations such as a stacked bar chart looking at the type of threat with respect to race and a heat map looking at how the victim fled the scene with respect to gender. I used protected attributes such as gender, race, and age in a decision tree algorithm to predict which city, state and county the shootings were most likely to occur in.

Marketing Plan for Apple Inc.

I wrote a comprehensive marketing plan for Apple Inc. where I showed how Apple has performed financially in the past 5 years, what their current marketing situation looks like, and the objectives and issues facing the company. I performed a detailed SWOT analysis on the company, assigning a rating and weight for each category. Then I discussed what actions Apple needs to take if they want to continue to succeed in the market, provided the budget needed to do so and what Apple can do to ensure they are on track to meet their goals.
